## Creative Direction Notes

The pig theme serves multiple purposes:
- **Memorable Branding**: "PIG" acronym makes the app unforgettable
- **Friendly Approach**: Makes photo organization feel approachable and fun
- **Professional Balance**: Modern, geometric pig design maintains software credibility
- **Portable Connection**: Pig as travel companion reinforces the portable nature
- **Market Differentiation**: Unique mascot sets it apart from generic gallery apps

Think of the pig as a friendly, professional assistant that helps you organize and carry your photos wherever you go!
